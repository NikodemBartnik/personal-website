About me

I'm a creative maker and a YouTuber. My work focuses on creating a huge variety of projects from 3D printing, through CNC machines to electronics and programming. I am also a business owner and I run a few online stores. I am a student at the Silesian University of Technology in Gliwice, Poland where I study data science and I am planning to finish my master's degree in 2024.

Some of the best projects I built so far are CNC machines IndyMill and Dremel CNC. These two machines have been built by many people all over the world and were not only fun to build for me but also turned out to be very useful in my workshop. I have also made a Mini Mars Rover and a few robots with Arduinos, LIDARs, and other sensors. In my work, I use a lot of 3D printing and I have a few 3D printers in my workshop. I also have a CNC machine and a laser cutter. I am also a big fan of open source and I try to make all of my projects open source.

I am not only focused on creating nice projects but also turning my passion into business. I am the founder and owner of Indystry.cc and ProsteCzęści.pl brands. Both brands are focused on online stores and over the years I have sold thousands of products all around the world.

My life

I was born in a city called Bytom in the south of Poland. From a young age, I have been fascinated by technology and always wanted to take things apart and see how they work. At the age of 13, I started playing with programming and setting up my first website. Not long after that I got my first Arduino and started to play with electronics and robots. From now on with every project I have been gaining some new knowledge and skills. I also like any kind of sport - regularly run and cycle. I don't know what the future will bring but I am excited to embrace it!

My Businesses and Projects

  • Indystry.cc

    Brand created to share my open source projects and sell the custom parts I make for them. Indystry was born as an idea of creating indepenedent open solutions similar to those used in the industry. Aiming at easy access, low entry cost and easy fixability. There is also indsytry.cc online store that is a big part of my business and allows me to invest in bigger and more comlicated projects. The biggest Indystry project so far is definitely the IndyMill CNC machine that was already built by thousands of users all around the world!

  • ProsteCzęści.pl

    YouTube channel and a website about DIY projects, technology and makin in Polish. I have been thinking about it for a very long time (about 3 years) and finnaly decided to give it a try! I was very suprised with the results and the positive feedback. The channel reached 10 000 subscribers in 7 months and is growing very rapidly. There are a lot of low quality/low effort YouTube channels in Poland that are very popular and I wanted to change that. The goal was to prove to others and myself that an inspiring, quality content that sparks curiosity and interest in science, technology and in general gaining knowledge can be not only popular but also interesting!

  • Open Robotic Platform

    OpenRoboticPlatform was born to simplify building robots. Simple, easy to follow design rules and a library of open parts make it a breeze to design and assemble your chassis. I have been slowly introducing the project in ym videos and developing more and more parts. My friend helped me a lot to design a really nice website and that's when the project was born for real! Over 100 users registered on the website and the library of parts is growing all the time!
